‎Workly is a decentralized freelance marketplace designed for the Pi Network’s 55+ million users. It addresses the lack of real-world utility for Pi tokens by providing a professional, borderless platform where "Pioneers" can monetize skills like coding, writing, and design without the banking barriers or high fees of traditional platforms like Fiverr.
‎
‎Core Features & Mechanics
‎Trustless Escrow: To eliminate scams, Pi tokens are locked in a secure vault upon hiring and only released to the freelancer once the client approves the final deliverables.
‎Sustainability Model: The platform maintains a 10% revenue model (5% client listing fee and 5% freelancer service fee) to fund operations and growth without needing fiat currency.
‎PiOS Integration: Built using the Pi Open Source license, the platform’s reputation and escrow logic are shared with the broader community to foster ecosystem growth.
‎
‎Strategic Impact
‎Workly transforms Pi from a speculative asset into a functional currency by increasing its velocity through high-frequency professional transactions. It fulfills the Pi Core Team’s vision of a self-sustaining, peer-to-peer economy by providing a secure environment for global talent exchange.
